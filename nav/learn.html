Identifying ALPRs

Flock ALPRs

Flock Safety cameras are typically mounted on dedicated poles, usually featuring a distinct solar panel above a dark, rectangular camera unit.

Ubicquia Smart Streetlights

Ubicquia devices are attached to existing streetlights. They typically appear as elongated modules with downward-facing camera lenses integrated directly into the light fixture.
