Learn how San Diego uses ALPRs

To learn more about ALPRs, see the ACLU and EFF publications.


The City of San Diego implements two notable forms of ALPR surveillance technology provided by Flock Safety and Ubicquia.

Flock ALPRs

Cameras are typically mounted on dedicated poles, usually featuring a distinct solar panel above a dark, rectangular camera unit.

Ubicquia Smart Streetlights

Cameras are attached to existing streetlights, typically appearing as elongated modules with downward-facing camera lenses integrated directly into the light fixture.
